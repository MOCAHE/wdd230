Site name

AZTECA Chamber of Commerce

Site purpose

Our purpose is to promote and defend the legitimate interests of Mexican entrepreneurs, support their growth through training and advice on legal matters, and facilitate national and international business development. As a chamber of commerce, we also provide networking opportunities and collaborations among our members, offer exclusive services and benefits such as discounts and networking events, and actively promote local trade, tourism, and investment in the region.

Color Schema

We prefer subdued colors and visually simple websites, and considering that a chamber of commerce warrants a website with those characteristics, I will use only black, white, gray, and a contrasting color for some visual details.

Typography

Due to the same reasons why we selected the color schema, we are looking for something sober and simple. Therefore, we will only choose one font style and apply different formats to it.
